# Garlic Bread

## Ingredients
- 1 baguette
- 1/2 cup butter (softened)
- 2 cloves garlic (minced)
- 1 tbsp parsley

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. Mix butter, garlic, and parsley.
3. Spread mixture on sliced baguette.
4. Bake for 10–12 minutes until golden.
